// LCOE FORMULA
LCOE = [ CAPEX + Σ(OPEX_t + Fuel_t) / (1+r)^t ] / [ Σ E_t / (1+r)^t ]
CAPEX = Capital Expenditure  |  r = Discount Rate  |  t = Year
E_t = Annual Energy Production (MWh)  |  OPEX = Annual Operating Cost
